Emily is originally from Hull and based in the North West. She is currently training at the Liverpool Institute for Performing Arts and will be graduating in 2016

About

I am training at the Liverpool Institute for Performing Arts and will be graduating in 2016.

I love working on heightened text and have a keen interest in television.

I am currently rehearsing the role of Lydia Litvyak in the new play (Fire) Embers (Ash) written and directed by Hailey Mashburn. Details to be announced soon.

I also work as a shadow puppeteer and an actor in community theatre in both mainstream and special schools.
