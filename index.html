About

I have been hooked on acting since my first role onstage in my home town of Hull.

I went on to complete the musical theatre course at the Oxford School of Drama, where I was the recipient of the Andrew Lloyd Webber scholarship, before training at LIPA.

At LIPA I have grown as an actress and I hope to continue building on the skills I've learned at drama school now as a graduate.
